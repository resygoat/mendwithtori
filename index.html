Our Story

Every child deserves moments of joy, especially during challenging times. What started as a simple idea has blossomed into a movement of compassion and creativity.

After being in the hospital myself, I created Mend With Tori to support kids who had gone through the same things I did, and provide a remedy for them.

I believe in the transformative power of art to bring comfort, spark imagination, and create meaningful connections for children facing medical challenges.

Through carefully curated activity kits, I bring tools for creative expression directly to pediatric patients, turning hospital rooms into studios of possibility.
